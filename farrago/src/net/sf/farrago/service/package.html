Contains services.

A service is a method call that can be accessed from any JDBC client. Generally a service is implemented by calling a UDX (user-defined transform) on the server, and often that UDX is in the {@code net.sf.farrago.syslib} package.

All classes in this package have very lightweight dependencies. Usually they just depend on JDBC. Sometimes they contain definitions that are used on the server; but they must not depend on server classes.
