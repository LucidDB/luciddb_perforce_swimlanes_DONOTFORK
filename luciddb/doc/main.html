LucidDB is an open-source RDBMS purpose-built for data warehousing and business intelligence. Most database systems (both proprietary and open-source) focus on transaction processing capabilities, and only bolt on analytical capabilities as an afterthought. By contrast, every component of LucidDB was designed with the requirements of flexible, high-performance data integration and sophisticated query processing in mind. Moreover, comprehensiveness within the focused scope of its architecture means simplicity for the user: no DBA required.
